About GPHA

GPHA sponsors six to eight shows each summer at a variety of locations in the Kansas City region. Hunter classes begin with jumps from ground poles to three feet. While hunter classes are the focus, GPHA does offer jumper classes in each show.

Entry fees are reasonable with a discount offered to members of the club. GPHA's emphasis is on fun, 'give back' and camaraderie. Winners are awarded ribbons and attractive prizes at each show while accumulating points toward year-end awards and ribbons.

We have an appointed board that governs over Great Plains Hunter Association.
