Become a Member

Membership in GPHA is open to anyone. Our annual membership year commences on January 1st of each year. Dues become payable in January and membership expires on December 31st. Anyone joining at any time during the membership year must pay the full amount on annual dues.

NOTE: GPHA does not issue membership cards.
